Heather Budney is a Professional Geologist and Master of Natural Resource Management (specialized in land use planning).

She has nine years of experience mapping sand and gravel deposits, and studying and evaluating sand and gravel regulations. Additionally, she has three years of experience writing and drafting for: conservation and reclamation plans for pits on private and public land, and applications for SMEs, SMLs, MLLs, VCEs and Water Act related matters.

Services:

Exploration and Evaluation:

  • Testing and mapping sand and gravel deposits
  • Estimating sand and gravel resource volumes

Regulatory Approvals:

  • Applications and maps for sand and gravel exploration permits (SMEs), and sand and gravel pits (SMLs, SMCs)
  • First Nations consultation related to sand and gravel pit applications
  • Conservation and reclamation plans for pits on private and public land
  • Municipal development permit applications for sand and gravel pits
  • Water Act applications related to sand and gravel pits
